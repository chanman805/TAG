The Gallery - Paintings

Your normal, typical landscape paintings. Nothing too glamorous or special here. Just me and the paints. Some of these might be for sale, contact me for details. It just depends how I feel about the particular painting, but I'll be sure to give dimensions if you ask. A lot of these are on the small side.

Here, I took some scenes that I had previously painted, and simply messed with the time a little. Hence the title, "the Other Twelve Hours". Seeing these same landscapes, often redrawn line-for-line, at a different time of day changes how you look at them. Gone are the familiar landmarks, all is shrouded in the abscence of light. Enjoy!

Here's what I call a hybrid between my comics and my paintings - the "Painted Sundays". I color these with watercolor, and a lot of these could be considered their own full-fledged paintings. I like to walk the line between types of art, and man do the critics and taxidermists have fun with that sort of behavior.
